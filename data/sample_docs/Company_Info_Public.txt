COMPANY VALUES AND GENERAL INFORMATION
Acme Enterprise Solutions
Public Document — Accessible to All Employees

OUR MISSION
To empower businesses worldwide with intelligent enterprise software that drives efficiency, growth, and innovation.

OUR VALUES

1. CUSTOMER FIRST
Every decision we make starts with the question: "How does this benefit our customer?" We listen deeply, respond quickly, and deliver beyond expectations.

2. INNOVATION WITHOUT BOUNDARIES
We encourage bold thinking and calculated risk-taking. Failure is acceptable when it leads to learning. We invest 15% of our R&D budget specifically in experimental projects with no guaranteed ROI.

3. INTEGRITY IN EVERYTHING
We do what we say and say what we mean. We operate with transparency internally and externally. Our financial reporting, performance feedback, and customer communications are always honest.

4. COLLABORATION OVER COMPETITION
We believe the best ideas come from diverse teams working together. Internal competition is discouraged. We celebrate team wins, not individual heroics.

5. CONTINUOUS LEARNING
All employees receive an annual learning budget of INR 50,000 for courses, certifications, and conferences. We host bi-weekly internal knowledge-sharing sessions called "Acme Academy."

COMPANY OVERVIEW

Founded in 2010, Acme Enterprise Solutions is a B2B SaaS company headquartered in Bengaluru, India, with offices in Singapore, London, and New York.

Number of employees: 3,200 (as of January 2024)
Annual Revenue: USD 450 million (FY2023)
Customers: 1,800+ enterprise customers across 45 countries
Industries served: Manufacturing, Financial Services, Healthcare, Retail, Technology
